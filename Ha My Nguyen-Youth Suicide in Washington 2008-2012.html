Youth Suicide Deaths in Washington State¶

By: Ha My Nguyen¶

1. Introduction¶

This project is going to explore the Youth Suicide Deaths in Washington State by Gender Age 0-17 Years, from 2008-2012. The json file is being collected from https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/youth-suicide-deaths-in-washington-state-by-gender-age-0-17-years-from-2008-2012.

Firstly , this report will explore the data by columns to get an idea of how the data look like then data will be converted into dictionary for further using purposes. Next, data cleaning will take in progress to get rid of unneccessary data. Afterwards, this report will perform different type of chart to compare the suicide rate by Year, Gender and County.

Finally, conclusion will be made with several recommendations.

3. Conclusion¶

The Youth Suicide Deaths in Washington State by Gender Age 0-17 Years, from 2008-2012 can be concluded as below

  • Whatcom has the lowest number of youth suicide in total while the total number of youth suicide in King district made the highest ranking.
  • The total number of youth suicide in Male is always higher than Female
  • The percentage of youth suicide is follows the increasing trend between 2009 and 2012. It was lowest in 2009 (14.7%) and reached the highest ranking in the year 2012 (27.9%).

4. Recommendation¶

The age from 0 to 17 years old is the time that teenager might face a lot of physical issue due to the change in their body, sexual and soul. The high rate of suicide technically gives an alert to all parents and government about lacking talented person in the future. Along with the rapidly development of society, rather than business, parents should spend more time with their children during this important growth up period and so does the government need to take into action of further investigation and creating new project of education strategy which helps teenagers have the right awareness of how important human wellbeing is and also how to deal with changes that challenge their life.
